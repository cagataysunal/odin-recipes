Menemen

Description

Making menemen isn’t hard, and it’s one of those dishes that always feels warm and comforting. With just a few simple ingredients — eggs, tomatoes, and peppers — you can create a tasty, traditional Turkish meal that’s perfect for 2-person breakfast or any time you want something easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil or butter
  • 2 green peppers, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil or butter in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped peppers and cook until they soften.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they release their juices.
  4. Crack the eggs into the pan and stir gently until cooked but soft.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper. Serve with bread.
